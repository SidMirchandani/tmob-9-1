Characters in The Mystery of Brahman

Bhrigu: A seeker of knowledge, son of Varuna, who embarks on a journey to understand Brahman through spiritual practice (tapas).

Varuna: The father of Bhrigu, who guides his son's spiritual inquiry through cryptic answers and encouragement to practice tapas.

Narrator: The voice that describes the story and explains concepts about Brahman that are not direct quotes from either character.
